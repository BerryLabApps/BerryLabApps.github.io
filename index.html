Game Alias. The goal of the game is to explain to your teammates as many words displayed on the screen of your device as it is possible for a limited time. Alias game also known as Taboo.

Select a category! Sets of words of different complexity and topics are suitable for any company.

Score points! Guess words and get points.

Win! The team that first scores the required number of points is the winner of the game.
